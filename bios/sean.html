Sean Zellmer

Hi, I'm Sean Zellmer. I believe logic is foundational to understanding and I want to understand everything: Theoretical Physics, Japanese caligraphy, you name it. Much like legos, my favorite childhood past-time, logic forms the building blocks from which a plethora of ideas can be composed. I want to explore those ideas. My favorite way of exploring is in the intersection of logical technology and seemingly illogical art. Art and technology, both dealing with making concepts manifest, can inform and play with one another. Out of this play-date comes a challenging of convention and a vision of new ideas. These ideas are Le Jeune Renard.
